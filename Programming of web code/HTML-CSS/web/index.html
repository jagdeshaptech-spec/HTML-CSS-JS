About Canva

Canva is a global online platform that empowers anyone to design anything and publish anywhere. With a simple drag-and-drop interface and a vast library of templates, photos, and fonts, Canva makes it easy for people and teams to create stunning visuals for work, school, or play — without needing professional design skills.

From presentations and social media posts to videos, business cards, and more — Canva helps millions of creators around the world bring their ideas to life beautifully and efficiently.
